Teil 3: Surveillance im IfSG --- class: center, middle, inverse # Teil 4: Probleme in Surveillance Systemen --- background-image: url({{ site.baseurl}}/assets/2021_10_28_CIEE/underreporting_pyramid.png) # Underreporting --- background-image: url({{ site.baseurl}}/assets/2021_10_28_CIEE/timeliness.png) # Zeitverzug --- # Falldefinition * norovirus causes gastroenteritis -- * case defintion: lab + gastroenteritis -- * lab reports come on their own -- * info on gastroenteritis must come from patient -- * if you dont ask the patient -> less cases -- Solutions tried: * Monitor lab reports as well * Analyze the system often --- # Typical problem: change * Surveillance systems dont like change * Example: people see less doctors --> less cases Solutions tried: * Create resilient systems --- # Kosten und Motivation * No reimbursment -> no reporting * Lots of reimbursment -> invented cases Solutions tried * Establish a strong legal system * Sentinal surveillance --- # Unbekanntes Event * New events are difficult to monitor * Example Anatoxin A Solutions tried: * invest in networks * event based surveillance --- # Nahezu-Elimination * nearly eliminated diseases increase false alarms * example measles: 80% false alarms Solutions tried: * adjust case definition --- class: center, middle, inverse #
