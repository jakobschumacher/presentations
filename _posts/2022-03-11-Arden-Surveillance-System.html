Part V: Problems --- background-image: url({{ site.baseurl}}/assets/2021_10_28_CIEE/underreporting_pyramid.png) # Typical problem: underreporting --- background-image: url({{ site.baseurl}}/assets/2021_10_28_CIEE/timeliness.png) # Typical problem: timeliness --- # Typical problem: event definition * norovirus causes gastroenteritis * case defintion: lab + gastroenteritis * lab reports come on their own * info on gastroenteritis must come from patient * if you dont ask the patient -> less cases Solutions tried: * Monitor lab reports as well * Analyze the system often --- # Typical problem: change * Surveillance systems dont like change * Example: people see less doctors --> less cases Solutions tried: * Create resilient systems --- # Typical problem: costs and motivation * No reimbursment -> no reporting * Lots of reimbursment -> invented cases Solutions tried * Establish a strong legal system * Sentinal surveillance --- # Typical problem: unkown event * New events are difficult to monitor * Example Anatoxin A Solutions tried: * invest in networks * event based surveillance --- # Typical problem: close to elimination * nearly eliminated diseases increase false alarms * example measles: 80% false alarms Solutions tried: * adjust case definition --- class: center, middle, inverse #
